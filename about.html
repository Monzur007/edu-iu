A digital e-learning platform is a software system for creating, delivering,
and managing online courses, offering features like content hosting, progress tracking,
assessments, and discussions, with examples ranging from massive open platforms (Coursera, edX)
and corporate tools (Docebo, Litmos) to school-focused systems (Google Classroom, Moodle)
and individual creator tools (Thinkific, Kajabi). These platforms make learning accessible anywhere,
anytime, supporting formal education, corporate training, and personal development.
